## üìà Economic Progression Model

### Early Game: Discovery & Onboarding (Phase 1)
- **Player Focus**: Learn, explore, earn freely
- **Token Economics**: Abundant STAR rewards, low burn costs
- **Key Features**:
  - 10-150 STAR per planet discovery
  - 10 STAR daily login rewards
  - Cheap utilities (25-50 STAR) encourage experimentation
  - NFT minting is free to incentivize collection

**Goal**: Rapid onboarding, exploration motivation

### Mid Game: Advancement & Strategy (Phase 2)
- **Player Focus**: Build collections, optimize earnings
- **Token Economics**: Balanced earning vs. burning
- **Key Features**:
  - Passive income from NFT ownership (0.5-0.75 STAR/hour)
  - Set bonuses reward completion (Inner 25, Outer 50, All 100 STAR)
  - Utilities cost more (75-150 STAR), require strategic decisions
  - Daily login streaks provide meaningful bonuses

**Goal**: Engagement through strategy, long-term play incentives

### Late Game: Mastery & Prestige (Phase 3)
- **Player Focus**: Governance, legacy, rare cosmetics
- **Token Economics**: Scarcity-driven, prestige-focused
- **Key Features**:
  - STAR becomes rare and valuable
  - Burning for cosmetics/rare NFTs becomes status symbol
  - DAO governance participation (100M tokens)
  - Community treasury voting rights
  - Exclusive prestige items only obtainable through burning

**Goal**: Long-term retention, community ownership
