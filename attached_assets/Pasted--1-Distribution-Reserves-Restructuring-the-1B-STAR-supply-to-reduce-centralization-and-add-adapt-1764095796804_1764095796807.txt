4. Smart Contract Modifications

- Dynamic Emission Control:  
  - Passive income rates tied to R_factor variable.  
  - DAO-controlled timelock adjusts rates only if inflation persists >3 months.  
  - Prevents runaway emissions without minting new supply.

- Transparent Vesting:  
  - 30% team allocation locked in contract.  
  - 12-month cliff + 48-month linear vesting.  
  - Publicly verifiable schedule.

- DAO-Controlled Burn Reserve:  
  - Burn reserve managed via smart contract.  
  - Release only by DAO vote, not team discretion.

- NFT Lifecycle Contracts:  
  - NFTs degrade over time.  
  - Require STAR burns for repair/maintenance.  
  - Adds repeatable sink tied to asset longevity.
