## Progression System

### Phase 1: Main Game (8 planets)
- Discover planets in order
- Earn tokens and NFTs
- Unlock set bonuses

### Phase 2: Dwarf Planet Hunt (7 optional)
- Access after Neptune
- Higher individual rewards
- Challenging to reach
- Prestigious collection

### Phase 3: Asteroid Mining (13 optional)
- Scattered throughout solar system
- Mini-collectibles
- Passive income stackable
- Market trading
