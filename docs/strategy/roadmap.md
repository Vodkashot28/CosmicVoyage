# Solar System Explorer - Development Roadmap

## Overview

This roadmap outlines the evolution of Solar System Explorer from MVP to a full-featured blockchain gaming platform. The project is structured in phases, each delivering value while building toward the long-term vision.

## Risk Mitigation

### Technical Risks
- **Smart contract vulnerabilities**: Regular audits, insurance
- **Performance issues**: Load testing, optimization roadmap
- **Data loss**: Backup systems, recovery procedures

### Market Risks
- **Low adoption**: Community-building initiatives, referral program
- **Token price volatility**: Staking/yield mechanisms
- **Competition**: Feature differentiation, user experience focus

### Regulatory Risks
- **Gaming regulation**: Compliance review, legal consultation
- **Crypto regulation**: Mainnet readiness, KYC/AML preparation
- **Data privacy**: GDPR compliance, privacy policy
